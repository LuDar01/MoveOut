ABOUT MOVEOUT

Welcome to MoveOut, your simple solution for organizing and managing belongings. With MoveOut, you can create custom labels, add content (images, audio, or text), and generate QR codes that display your items when scanned. After a quick registration with email verification (or skipping it with Gmail), you can easily choose a label design and assign content to your boxes. MoveOut automatically generates printable QR codes, making content accessible to anyone who scans it. For added privacy, labels can be protected by a 6-digit PIN. Users can also delete or deactivate their profiles, with admin oversight for account management. Inactive accounts are deactivated after a month with reminders sent beforehand. At MoveOut, simplicity and efficiency are at the core of our design, ensuring you can manage your items with ease.
